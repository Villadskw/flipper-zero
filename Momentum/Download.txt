How do i download Momentum Firmware?

1. Make sure to have qFlipper (https://flipperzero.one/update)

2. Open https://github.com/Next-Flip/Momentum-Firmware/releases/tag/mntm-001

3. Download firmware using "qFlipper Package (.tgz)"

4. Open qFlipper and connect your Flipper Zero (With cables)

5. Click "Install from file", and find the file you just downloaded (Step 3)

6. Wait

7. You now have Momentum Firmware
